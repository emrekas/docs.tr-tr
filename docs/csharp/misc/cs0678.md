 <span data-ttu-id="96ed3-104">[Geçici](../language-reference/keywords/volatile.md) ve [salt okunur](../language-reference/keywords/readonly.md) anahtar sözcüklerin kullanımı birbirini dışlıyor.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="96ed3-104">Use of the [volatile](../language-reference/keywords/volatile.md) and [readonly](../language-reference/keywords/readonly.md) keywords is mutually exclusive.</span></span>

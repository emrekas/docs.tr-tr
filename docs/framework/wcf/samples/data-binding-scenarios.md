 [Windows Forms İstemcisinde Veri Bağlama](../../../../docs/framework/wcf/samples/data-binding-in-a-windows-forms-client.md)

 [Bir ASP.NET İstemcisinde Veri Bağlama](../../../../docs/framework/wcf/samples/data-binding-in-an-aspnet-client.md)

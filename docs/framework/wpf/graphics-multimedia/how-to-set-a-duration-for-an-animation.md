<span data-ttu-id="915ad-103">A <xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.Timeline> zaman bir segmentini ve kesiminin uzunluğu çizelgesinin tarafından belirlenir temsil <xref:System.Windows.Duration>.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915ad-103">A <xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.Timeline> represents a segment of time and the length of that segment is determined by the timeline's <xref:System.Windows.Duration>.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915ad-104">Olduğunda bir <xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.Timeline> geçerlilik süresinin, yürütmeyi durdurur.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915ad-104">When a <xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.Timeline> reaches the end of its duration, it stops playing.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915ad-105">Varsa <xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.Timeline> alt öğe zaman çizelgeleri varsa bunlar da yürütmeyi durdurur.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915ad-105">If the <xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.Timeline> has child timelines, they stop playing as well.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="915ad-106">Bir animasyon, söz konusu olduğunda <xref:System.Windows.Duration> animasyon bitiş değeri başlangıç değerinden geçişin ne kadar alacağını belirtir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="915ad-106">In the case of an animation, the <xref:System.Windows.Duration> specifies how long the animation takes to transition from its starting value to its ending value.</span></span>
